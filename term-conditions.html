Activation of Membership: The Membership is automatically activated the next day as per the date payment of membership fees unless specified by the member on sign up. The club cannot hold the membership starting date more than 30 days.

Transfer: Transfer of a membership is permitted to a nonmember i.e. to a person who has not been a member with the VC fitness Gym. Transfer fees of 1000+ taxes per transfer is applicable.

Extension: WE DO NOT extend membership. Extension is only given in one to one training on a medical basis for submission of an application & all relevant medical documents. Cancellation / Refund: No refund shall be made for membership dues paid, except as follows:

If the reason of death or permanent disability, the member is unable to use the services or facilities of the gym. The term “Disability’’ means a condition which the member is unable to use facility & and “Permanent’’ means more than six months. No refund will be given for temporary basis.

DECLARATION: You declare that you are in a physically sound state of health and are not suffering from any condition, impairment, disease, infirmity or other illness that would prevent your participation or use of equipment or machinery in the gym. Use of the Facilities: The facility shall be used for training purposes only. No other activity will be allowed, and violation will be sanctioned. For example, sleeping and lingering are not allowed. Code of Conduct: The member shall respect the behavioral and operating principles and follow them on all the occasions. This includes, but not limited to:

  • Wearing appropriate and clean clothes and shoes.
  • Using towel at every workout and leaving machines and surfaces clean after usage.
  • Behaving in a friendly and respectful manner towards the other members in the gym as well as the staff.

Lockers for Personal Belongings: Lockers are available for members while working out in the facility. Each member needs to empty the locker and keep the keys while leaving the facility. VC Fitness may empty lockers if member is not in the facility.

Lost Property or Theft: VC Fitness does not take responsibility for the loss or theft of any personal belongings, even if locked in a locker.

Suspension/Termination of Membership: If a member behaves in a way that breaches the code of conduct, interferes or disturbs other members or staff, or that management considers inappropriate the membership can be suspended or terminated at management’s sole discretion. If management does not pay membership fees, the membership can be suspended or terminated at management’s sole discretion.

Price Changes All the prices are fixed for the duration of the contract. A price change can be implemented by the management from time to time and will be updated on the brochure of the gym. In case of price change, member has to pay the revised price on renewal of the contract.
